What is OtoWA?

OtoWA is a simple WhatsApp automation and broadcast tool. It allows you to broadcast personalized WhatsApp messages to your contacts. You can use it to engage inactive users, send event reminders, and more.

Features

Broadcast Messages

Broadcast personalized WhatsApp messages to your contacts.

Send on Autopilot

Broadcast personalized messages to your entire contact list with just one click.

Schedule Messages

Schedule your WhatsApp messages to be sent at a specific time.

Personalized Attachments

Send personalized images or files to your contacts.

Send via Phone

Messages are sent from your own WhatsApp number, making them authentic.

Technical Set-up

Technical support to help you set up and get started.

  • Engage inactive users or customers who haven't purchased from you in a while.
